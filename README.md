# The list of professorships and research groups in engineering geodesy
You wish to add or change something? Please send me an [email](mailto:mvarga1989@gmail.com), create a [pull request](https://github.com/mvarga1989/Engineering_geodesy_professorships/pulls) or an [issue](https://github.com/mvarga1989/Engineering_geodesy_professorships/issues).

- [**Research Division Engineering Geodesy**, TU Vienna](https://ig.geo.tuwien.ac.at/)
- [**Institute of Engineering Geodesy and Measurement Systems**, TU Graz](https://www.tugraz.at/en/institute/igms/home)
- [**Geosensors and Engineering Geodesy**, ETH Zürich](https://gseg.igp.ethz.ch/)
- [**Geodätisches Institut**, Leibniz Universität Hannover](https://www.gih.uni-hannover.de/de/)
- [**Institut für Geodäsie und Photogrammetrie**, TU Braunschweig](https://www.tu-braunschweig.de/igp)
- [**Chair of Engineering Geodesy and Geodetic Laboratory**, TU München](https://gseg.igp.ethz.ch/)
- [**Institut für Geodäsie und Geoinformation**, Universität Bonn](https://www.gib.uni-bonn.de/)
- [**Institut für Ingenieurgeodäsie**, Universität Stuttgart](https://www.iigs.uni-stuttgart.de/)
